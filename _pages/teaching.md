Teaching and mentorship are central to my academic identity — spanning 20+ years of 
classroom instruction, doctoral supervision, and junior faculty development across 
Montana State University and Georgia State University.

## Program Direction

I am the **Founder and Director** of the **Big Data & Machine Learning (BDML) 
Concentration** within the M.S. in Data Science and Analytics program at GSU (2019–present), 
one of the fastest-growing graduate concentrations at the university. Previously, I served 
as **PhD Program Director** in the Department of Computer Science at Montana State University 
(2006–2013).
